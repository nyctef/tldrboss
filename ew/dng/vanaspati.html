Terminus Snatcher

Mouth Off
Stand on one of the closed mouths
What is Left/Right
  • Half-room AoE from the boss's left or right.
  • The boss will raise one arm as a telegraph.
  • Sometimes covers a closed mouth - move to the other one.
Lost Hope
Temporary Misdirection. You have time, so carefully make your way to a closed mouth.

Terminus Wrecker

Unholy Water -> Aether Siphon -> Aether Spray
  • Take note of the water spheres around the edge of the arena.
  • Water siphon (knockback marker) -> position to avoid getting knocked back into the water.
  • Fire siphon (roomwide AoE) -> stand in the water spheres before the cast finishes.
    • get into the spheres with plenty of time left! They can be finicky.

Blasphemy: Svarbhanu

Aetherial Disruption -> Crumbling Sky
  • Room will be divided into red circle / blue triangle sections.
  • A red circle or blue triangle will flash on screen.
  • Stand on the opposite marker to avoid large damage.
  • Will be combined with knockbacks and PbAoEs later.
Meteor phase
  • After a while, the boss will become untargetable and move to one end of the arena.
  • Keep an eye on the boss in the sky to see three meteor telegraphs with one gap.
  • Move to the column of the arena without a meteor, then avoid AoEs.
