Traps:

Traps are invisible locations on the ground between bosses that may trigger large amounts of damage or debuffs. The Lost Perception action can spot traps, or a tank with Lost Swift and invulns can trigger them safely.

Trinity Seeker

Verdant Path
Switches between different sets of abilities: Katanas, Greatsword or Bare-handed.
Katanas: Act of Mercy
A cross-shaped AoE from the boss's hitbox
Katanas: Mercy
  • Stand in the middle of the boss's hitbox.
  • Watch the four glowing katanas to memorize four half-room AoEs.
  • The lines of the AoEs are on the boss's hitbox intercardinals.
Katanas: Seasons of Mercy
  • Avoid the criss-cross AoEs.
  • Look away from the gaze marker.
  • Move away from the growing purple marker.
Greatsword: Baleful Swath
Get close to the boss to avoid some large AoEs.
Greatsword: Burning Chains
Break tethers between players.
Greatsword: Baleful Blade and Phantom Edge
  • Move towards the barricades at the edge of the arena.
  • If there's a knockback marker, then stay in front of the barricades.
  • Otherwise, hide behind the barricades.
Barehanded: Iron Impact
Line stack marker on a random player with large amounts of damage.
Barehanded: Iron Splitter
The boss will punch a ring of one color. Move onto the opposite color.
Barehanded: Dead Iron
Spread out around the boss to avoid player-targeted cone AoEs.
