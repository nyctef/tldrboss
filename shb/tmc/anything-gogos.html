Required spells:

  • White Wind (or other large amounts of healing)
  • Flying Sardine (for interrupting a cast in act 1)
  • Diamondback (to survive the third meteor in act 2)
  • Exuviation (to remove heavy debuffs in act 2)

Act 1

  • Mimic: do not attack Gogo while he has the Mimicry buff.
  • Mimicked Sap: Evade the first AoE, then keep running for two more.
  • Mimicked Imp Song: Interrupt the cast with Flying Sardine.
  • Mimicked Doom Impeding: Get full health before the cast completes.
  • Mimicked Bunshin: Avoid some more AoEs
  • Mimicked Raw Instinct: Extra damage. Can be ignored, or removed with Eerie Soundwave.

Act 2

  • Gogo Fire III: Move away from the boss during the cast, then cease all action when you get the Pyretic debuff.
  • Gogo Blizzard III: Point-blank AoE on the boss.
  • Gogo Thunder III: Is cast next. Leaves a damaging puddle; can be baited towards the edge of the arena.
  • Gogo Flare -> Gogo Holy: Heal up after each.
  • Gogo Meteor: Move away from the first proximity marker. Dodge random AoEs. Move to the center, and cast Diamondback before or as the second meteor lands to tank the third.
    ⚠ Warning: stay out of melee range of Gogo while casting Diamondback, or he might interrupt it!
  • Charybdis + Icestorm: Avoid two, then four large persistent AoEs at the edges. Cleanse two debuffs immediately, then move into a gap between tornados.
    ⚠ Warning: the tornado AoEs are slightly larger than the visuals! Give them plenty of space.
  • Gogo Thunder III: Now fires three times in succession. Bait the AoEs into the gaps between the tornados. Move away into the remaining space to avoid another Gogo Fire + Blizzard combo.
