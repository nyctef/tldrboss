Cetus

Generic mechanics
Various AoEs to avoid and adds to kill.
Electric Whorl
After the three Hybodus adds are dead, Cetus will Body Slam to a random point, then Electric Whorl a donut AoE.

Irminsul and Sawtooth

Resistance buffs
  • Each boss will be invulnerable to either Ranged DPS or Magic (Caster/Healer) attacks.
  • Make sure your attacks are doing damage!
Mucus Bomb
  • Players hit by the AoE will be tethered together and stunned.
  • Other players can run through the tethers to break them.
Shockwave Stomp
Cast by Sawtooth. Hide behind Irminsul to avoid fatal damage.

Cuchulainn

Corrosive Bile
Lingering poison AoE. Avoid standing in front of the boss.
Flailing Tentacles
Raised tentacles will land with line AoEs on diagonals.
Void Pact -> Beckon -> Devour
Pull adds away from the boss's front so it can't Devour them.
Bile Below
Once the second wave of Foobar adds have spawned, stand on the raised platforms.
Black Lung
Touch the orange orbs before they explode.

Echidna

Generic mechanics
Watch out for gaze attacks.
Sickle Slash
Large AoEs to left and right. Safe line down the center.
Demonic Descent
Prioritise the stack marker if one appears.
Snake adds: Echidna/Sinister/Dexter
These adds need to be pulled apart. Watch out for more gaze attacks.
Bloodguard adds
Just burn these ones down.
