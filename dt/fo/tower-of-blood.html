Raidplans

General: XX Expulsion Afar / XX Dangers Near
  • Expulsion Afar: knockback, hug the boss
  • Dangers Near: point-blank AoE, get at least halfway along the arena away from the boss
Demonic Dark II
  • Raidwide
Ray of Expulsion/Dangers x2
  • Current side of the boss will become unsafe
  • Run through boss and resolve Afar/Near
Occult Chisel
  • Tankbusters on three highest enmity targets
  • Tanks hug boss, everyone else back up a bit
Demonograph of Expulsion/Dangers
  • Numbers groups run through the boss, letters groups stay
  • Resolve Afar/Near
  • Get into the appropriate stacks
Rotate Left/Right x2
  • Stack up near the boss on the side opposite the castbar name
  • Be behind the boss for the second cast
Cometeor of Expulsion/Dangers
  • Check which side the meteor marker is on
  • If you have:
    • No marker: get on the opposite side of the boss from the meteor
    • A meteor marker: get on the same side as the meteor
    • A stack marker pointing away from the meteor: same side
    • A stack marker pointing towards the meteor: opposite side
  • Resolve Afar/Near
  • Then:
    • Meteors: Drop as far away from the boss as possible
    • Meteor side stacks: jump over the boss
    • Non-meteor side stacks: get on the opposite edge
Repeats: Rotate Left/Right x2

Summon -> Demonography -> Gravity of Expulsion/Dangers
  • Everyone runs to the side of the boss with towers
  • Resolve Afar/Near
  • Numbers groups: stand with the statues on the sides to float
  • Get into assigned towers, and look for the furthest point from adds
  • Once towers proc, move into furthest corner for the safe spot
