Boss 1: Demon Tablet

Raidplans
  • ABBA+: w8wd3ca3br4kx2w6
General: XX Expulsion Afar / XX Dangers Near
  • Expulsion Afar: knockback, hug the boss
  • Dangers Near: point-blank AoE, get at least halfway along the arena away from the boss
Demonic Dark II
  • Raidwide
Ray of Expulsion/Dangers x2
  • Current side of the boss will become unsafe
  • Run through boss and resolve Afar/Near
Occult Chisel
  • Tankbusters on three highest enmity targets
  • Tanks hug boss, everyone else back up a bit
Demonograph of Expulsion/Dangers
  • Numbers groups run through the boss, letters groups stay
  • Resolve Afar/Near
  • Get into the appropriate stacks
Rotate Left/Right x2
  • Stack up near the boss on the side opposite the castbar name
  • Be behind the boss for the second cast
Cometeor of Expulsion/Dangers
  • Check which side the meteor marker is on
  • If you have:
    • No marker: get on the opposite side of the boss from the meteor
    • A meteor marker: get on the same side as the meteor
    • A stack marker pointing away from the meteor: same side
    • A stack marker pointing towards the meteor: opposite side
  • Resolve Afar/Near
  • Then:
    • Meteors: Drop as far away from the boss as possible
    • Meteor side stacks: jump over the boss
    • Non-meteor side stacks: get on the opposite edge
Repeats: Rotate Left/Right x2
Summon -> Adds
  • Numbers group run through the boss
  • Time Mage: dispel the central add
  • Everyone else kill the side adds
Summon -> Demonography -> Gravity of Expulsion/Dangers
  • Everyone runs to the side of the boss with towers
  • Resolve Afar/Near
  • Numbers groups: stand with the statues on the sides to float
  • Get into assigned towers, and look for the furthest point from adds
  • Once towers proc, move into furthest corner for the safe spot
Repeats:
  • Rotate Left/Right x2
  • Cometeor of Expulsion/Dangers
  • Ray of Expulsion/Dangers
  • Occult Chisel
  • Demonic Dark II
  • End of History (10s enrage)

Boss 2: Nereid, Triton and Phobos

Raidplans
  • ABBA+: r8jLeQdSo_LLfm4w
Decisive Battle
Stack up inside assigned boss' hitbox.
Slice 'n' Dice
Tankbuster cones: aim outside the arena
Three-body Problem -> Primordial Chaos -> Frozen Fallout -> Noxious Nova
  • Each player will randomly get 1-3 stacks of fire or ice
  • Red/blue slimes will telegraph four large AoEs each
  • Plan to get hit by enough AoEs of the opposite color, then stand in safe spots
  • Ending up with zero stacks is required to survive Noxious Nova
Vengeful Fire/Blizzard/Bio III -> Delta Attack
  • Stack up on the boss after resolving the previous mechanic
  • As soon as the two adds jump, run to the green tiled area between them and close to the wall
  • Fast reflexes or Sprint is recommended
  • Delta Attack is a hard-hitting triple raidwide
Firestrike
Party line stacks: aim outwards
Snowball Fight -> Chilling Collision -> Avalaunch -> To The Wind -> Self-destruct
  • Hold burst for a moment
  • Numbers and Letters groups are assigned to snowballs based on the arena markers
  • Each snowball telegraphs three line stacks
  • Groups take stacks in order (1->2->3, A->B->C)
  • Stacks are Wild Charges (tanks in front)
  • Take a note of where your snowball ends up
  • If you have a tether: get knocked back opposite your tether
  • If you don't: get knocked back opposite your assigned snowball
  • Burst down snowballs to avoid enrage
Repeats: Vengeful Fire/Blizzard/Bio III -> Delta Attack
Repeats: Slice 'n' Dice + Firestrike (simultaneous)
Aim tankbuster to the left, and party line stack to the right. Keep both away from the other parties.
Three-Body Problem -> Elemental Impact -> Geothermal Ruin -> Flamethrower
  • Watch for "footsteps" on the ground
  • Numbers groups take footsteps that start at numbers markers, and vice-versa
  • Group up around the assigned fireball
  • DPS stack on the ground line going to the wall
  • Healers stack on either side of the fireball
  • OTs take the first tower (mit first hit, invuln the second two)
  • Everyone stack with the DPS, then bait AoEs clockwise (moving left facing the fireball)
  • Stay grouped while moving to take a line stack
  • Now MTs take the tower (mit one hit then invuln)
Repeats: Vengeful Fire/Blizzard/Bio III
Six-Handed Fistfight -> Collateral Damage -> Fusion Burst
  • Stand next to originally asigned boss
  • Dodge pizza slice AoEs
  • Spread for Collateral Damage
  • Fusion Burst is enrage
