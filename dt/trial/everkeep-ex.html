Guide (Hector)

Intro

Multidirectional Divide
  • Thin line AoEs to dodge
  • Thick and thin lines left behind will telegraph future line AoEs to dodge
  • Smallish checkerboard safespots in each quadrant
Forward Half
  • Be north of the boss's hitbox
  • Head to the side of the area WITH the glowing sword
  • Dodge the telegraphed line AoEs
Backward Half
  • Be south of the boss's hitbox
  • Head to the side of the area WITHOUT the glowing sword
  • Dodge the telegraphed line AoEs
Regicidal Rage
  • Healers + DPS stay put from resolving the F/B Half
  • Tanks take tankbuster tethers to sides of the boss
  • Dodge a new set of line AoEs as the tankbusters resolve
Dawn of an Age
Raidwide + transition
Vollok -> Sync -> Half Full
  • Swords appear on copied platforms
  • Glowing swords will damage the matching tiles on the main platform
  • Also dodge the boss's glowing sword side
  • TIP: non-glowing swords will telegraph safe places to stand
Greater Gateway -> Forged Track
  • Regular portals make a line of the arena unsafe
  • Fire portals expand a single line to cover three lines
  • Wind portals create a perpendicular knockback (immunity works)
Pyramid markers above players
Spread so that one person is on each tile
Actualize
Raidwide + transition
Projection of Triumph
  • A grid of swords appear, plus moving lines with point-black or donut AoEs
  • The symbols on each line causes the corresponding AoE when it hits a sword
  • A second Projection of Triumph cast will make a second set of moving lines
  • After the third set of AoEs fire, the boss will cast Forward or Backward Half
  • Deal with the fourth set of ground AoEs while moving to resolve F/B Half
  • Deal with the fifth set of AoEs immediately after F/B Half
Actualize -> Projection of Turmoil (Congaline strat)
  • Position according to pre-set markers
  • Tanks use heavy mit to absorb the first set of hits solo
  • After each player gets hit, they start following the stack line to share damage with the following players
  • M1/M2 will also need some fairly serious mitigation but the damage gets lighter over time
Bitter Whirlwind
  • Three-hit AoE tankbuster on the MT
  • Either swap tanks between hits or invuln
  • WAR can invuln both instances of this attack
Dawn of an Age
Raidwide + transition again
Drum of Vollok
  • Line up with partners
  • Players targeted move closer to the boss
  • Partners line up exactly to be knocked onto the next platform
  • After knockback: Group up towards the gap between platforms
Vollok -> Sync
  • Big swords this time that make matching quadrants unsafe
  • Spread within the one safe tile that is in melee range
  • Resolve pyramid markers as before with players on separate tiles
  • Move away from the new wind AoEs
Forward/Backward Half
If necessary, use the wind AoE to bounce yourself onto the platform with the safe space
Duty's Edge
HEAVY damage line stack marker: mitigate and heal
Burning Chains
  • Start stacked up
  • Roles who didn't take the wind bumper before, do so now
  • Everyone move out of melee range of the boss to break the chains
Actualize
One more raidwide + transition
Projection of Triumph
Start resolving this as before
Half Circuit
  • Boss telegraphs glowing sword for an unsafe side,
  • plus two swords on the ground for donut or PBAoE
  • Resolves just after the third set of ground AoEs
  • Try to stay together as a group so that...
Regicidal Rage
...the tanks can pick up these tethers as before
Projection of Turmoil
Congaline, as before, with extra Half Full casts to dodge
Bitter Whirlwind
Other tank invulns
